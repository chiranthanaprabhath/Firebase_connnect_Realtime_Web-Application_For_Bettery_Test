Rated Capacity

Rated Capacity

Please Follow The Below Steps
  1. Charge the battray according to the method declared by the manufacuturer.
  2. Store the battary in ambient temperature of 20°C ± 5°C for not less than 1hour and not more than 4hours.
  3. Discharge the battery in ambient temperature of 20°C ± 5°C at a constant current of 0.2ItA until the battery voltage is less than or equal to the specified end of discharge voltage.
